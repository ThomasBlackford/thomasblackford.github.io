Website Evaluations

Lichess

Lichess is a free, open-source online chess platform that emphasizes minimalism and accessibility, making it extremely user friendly, even on low end systems

  • Contrast: Clear differentiation between game elements and background. Easy to see board and pieces.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, buttons, and layout across all pages, predictable user interface.
  • Alignment: Boards, menus, and info panels are aligned neatly. Everything feels organized.
  • Proximity: Related elements like player info, moves, and chat are grouped logically, improving cohesion.
